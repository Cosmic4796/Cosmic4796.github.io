Sample Commands

Fun.Bot offers over 100+ commands! Here’s a preview of some popular ones (all use /):

  • /trivia – Play a trivia game with your server members.
  • /meme – Get a random meme from the internet.
  • /remind [time] [message] – Set a personal reminder.
  • /daily – Claim daily coins and rewards.
  • /profile [user] – View your or another member’s profile and stats.
