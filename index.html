Amazing Features

Virtual Economy

Complete currency system with earning, spending, and trading capabilities. Users can accumulate wealth through various activities and compete on leaderboards.

Jobs System

Users can work different jobs with varying pay rates and cooldowns. Customize jobs to fit your server's theme with unique names and salaries.

Gambling Games

Multiple gambling options including slots, roulette, blackjack, and coin flips with configurable odds. Set limits to prevent excessive gambling.

Fishing Mini-Game

Interactive fishing with rare catches, upgrades, and a leaderboard system. Different fish have different values and rarity levels.

Lottery System

Regular lottery draws with configurable ticket prices and jackpot accumulation. Winners take home massive payouts from the pooled tickets.

Item Shop

Purchase items, roles, or special privileges using your earned currency. Server admins can customize the shop with unique offerings.

Beta Release Announcement

We're excited to announce the beta release of Fun.Bot! After months of development and testing, we're ready to share our economy bot with the Discord community. This initial release includes all core features:

  • Complete virtual economy system with currency
  • Working jobs with cooldowns and varied payouts
  • Multiple gambling games (slots, coin flip, etc.)
  • Fishing mini-game with rare catches
  • Lottery system with growing jackpots
  • Item shop with useful upgrades

As this is a beta release, we anticipate some bugs and issues. We appreciate your patience and feedback as we work to improve the bot. Special rewards will be given to early adopters who help identify issues during the beta period!

Note: Economy data may be reset during the beta period as we make balance adjustments.
